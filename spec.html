1. Executive Summary

Centime Expense is a corporate expense management solution built in partnership with Findity (API provider) with Centime delivering the user experience. It is part of the broader Centime suite, with reimbursement payments handled by Centime AP.

Card Interchange Revenue

Free with Centime-issued or partner cards. Per-user fees for third-party cards.

Card Agnostic

Any Visa/Mastercard corporate or personal card supported.

Bank Partner Friendly

White-label ready for bank partners and resellers.

Direct + Channel

Sell direct to mid-market and through bank partner channels.

Speed of Implementation

Rapid onboarding and deployment for customers.

Private Label

Full branding support for partners and resellers.

4. Core Features

4.1 Expense Reporting

4.1.1 Expense Entry
  • Manual expense creation with all standard fields (date, merchant, amount, currency, category, description)
  • Multi-currency support with automatic exchange rate lookup
  • Itemized expenses (e.g., hotel folio line items)
  • Mileage tracking with GPS and map-based route entry
  • Per diem calculations (GSA rates and custom rates)
  • Recurring expenses
  • Duplicate detection and warnings
  • Split expenses across multiple cost centers, projects, or GL codes
  • Personal expense flagging
  • Attendee tracking for meals and entertainment (names, titles, business purpose)
  • Billable expense flagging (mark expenses as client-billable)
4.1.2 Expense Reports
  • Group expenses into reports for submission
  • Report templates (by trip, by period, by project)
  • Pre-populated reports from card transactions
  • Auto-submit rules for low-value / policy-compliant expenses
  • Draft, submitted, approved, rejected, paid statuses
  • Report cloning and templates
  • Batch operations (select multiple expenses, bulk categorize, bulk submit)
4.1.3 Delegates and Assistants
  • Expense entry delegation (executive assistants can submit on behalf of others)
  • Configurable delegation permissions

4.2 Receipt Management

4.2.1 Receipt Capture
  • Mobile camera capture with real-time edge detection and image enhancement
  • Email forwarding (receipts@[tenant].centime-expense.com)
  • Drag-and-drop upload on web
  • Bulk receipt upload
  • E-receipt integrations (Uber, Lyft, airlines, hotels)
  • SMS/text message receipt forwarding
  • Slack and Microsoft Teams receipt submission
  • Lost receipt affidavit/declaration workflow
4.2.2 Receipt Processing (OCR/AI)
  • Automatic extraction: merchant name, date, total amount, currency, tax, tip, line items
  • Multi-language receipt support
  • Confidence scoring on extracted data
  • Auto-categorization based on merchant
  • Receipt-to-transaction matching
4.2.3 Receipt Storage
  • Unlimited storage · Original image preservation · PDF, JPEG, PNG
  • Receipt audit trail (who uploaded, when, modifications)
  • IRS-compliant receipt archival

4.3 Corporate Card Management

4.3.2 Real-Time Transaction Feed
  • Visa RTN — Authorization-level data in real-time
  • Mastercard Notifications — Real-time push of card transactions
  • Automatic posting to user accounts
  • Pending vs. settled states · Merchant enrichment (names, logos, MCC) · Declined visibility
4.3.3 Receipt-Transaction Matching
  • Auto-match by amount, date, and merchant with confidence scoring
  • Smart matching using GPS proximity
  • Unmatched transaction alerts and receipt suggestions
4.3.4 Card Controls (Phase 2)
  • Spend limits (per transaction, daily, monthly) · MCC restrictions · Geographic restrictions
  • Card freeze/unfreeze · Virtual card issuance

4.4 Policy Engine

4.4.1 Policy Rules
  • Spending limits by category, role, department, project
  • Receipt requirements (threshold-based)
  • Duplicate detection · Weekend/holiday rules
  • Preferred vendor enforcement
  • Per diem limits · Mileage rate caps
  • Personal expense restrictions
  • Out-of-policy: warnings vs. hard blocks (configurable)
  • Attendee requirements for meals (threshold-based)
  • Itemization requirements
4.4.2 Policy Configuration
  • No-code policy builder UI
  • Multiple policy sets (entity, dept, role, location)
  • Policy inheritance and overrides
  • Policy effective dates
  • Audit log for policy changes

4.5 Approval Workflows

4.5.1 Workflow Configuration
  • Multi-level approval chains
  • Approval by: manager, cost center, project, custom
  • Amount-based routing with auto-approve
  • Sequential and parallel paths
  • Escalation rules and timeout handling
  • Out-of-office delegation / backup approvers
4.5.2 Approver Experience
  • Approve/reject with comments
  • Line-item or full-report approval
  • Send back for revision
  • Batch approvals
  • Mobile push · Email digest
  • Full approval audit trail

4.6 Reimbursement

4.6.1 Out-of-Pocket
  • Integration with Centime AP for payment processing
  • ACH direct deposit to employee bank accounts
  • Payment status tracking (pending, processing, paid)
  • Multi-currency reimbursement
  • Payroll integration option (Phase 2)
4.6.2 Card Reconciliation
  • Auto-reconciliation of approved expenses vs. card statement
  • Unreconciled transaction reporting
  • Statement-level sign-off

4.7 Collaboration & Messaging

In-App
  • Comments and notes on expenses/reports
  • @mention team members
  • Conversation threads (submitter ↔ approver)
  • Attachment support
Third-Party Integrations
  • Slack — Notifications, receipt submission, approve/reject
  • Microsoft Teams — Notifications, receipt submission, approve/reject
  • Configurable bot commands

4.8 Purchase Requisitions & PO Creation

Vendor management, PO matching, and downstream processes handled by Centime AP.

4.8.1 Purchase Requisitions
  • Employee-initiated with item details, quantity, cost, justification
  • Catalog-based and free-form requests
  • Budget check at request time
  • Link to projects, departments, cost centers
  • Request templates for frequent items
  • Status tracking (draft → submitted → approved → PO)
4.8.2 Requisition Approvals
  • Multi-level approval chains
  • Amount-based routing
  • Sequential and parallel paths
  • Auto-approve for catalog items below threshold
  • Escalation rules · Audit trail
4.8.3 PO Creation & Posting
  • Auto-generate POs from approved requisitions
  • Configurable PO numbering
  • Post to ERP via Centime GL APIs
  • Email PO to vendor
  • Change orders with re-approval
4.8.4 Budget Management
  • Department and project budgets (monthly, quarterly, annual) · Real-time consumption (committed + spent) · Alerts at 75%, 90%, 100%
  • Budget vs. actual reporting · Multi-level hierarchy (company → division → dept → project)

4.9 Tax Compliance (Phase 2)

  • VAT/GST capture on receipts (OCR extraction)
  • Tax reclaim workflow · Tax code mapping to ERP
  • Country-specific rules · Tax summary reports

4.10 Travel (Phase 4 — Future)

  • Travel booking integration
  • Pre-trip approval workflows
  • Itinerary management
  • Travel policy enforcement at point of booking
